The Spiritual Foundation of Shivdhenu

Tej Pratibimb Peeth Shri Rudra Kesari Math, established in Belagavi in 2011 under the divine blessings of Jagadguru Siddharoodha Maharaj, has long been dedicated to devotion, service, and the protection of Sanatana Dharma.

Inspired by this mission and rooted in Gou Seva, Shivdhenu was created to bring pure, traditional food into every home while enabling every purchase to support the welfare of indigenous Gir cows.

Shivdhenu is more than a brand—it is a mission of purity, tradition, and service.

The Name

What Shivdhenu Means

Shiv — the auspicious, the pure, the source of all good in the universe.

Dhenu — the sacred cow, Kamadhenu, the divine mother who is the source of all abundance in Vedic tradition.

Together, Shivdhenu represents our deepest intention: to bring food that is sacred, pure and abundant — as nature and our ancestors intended.

"माँ का आशीर्वाद, प्रकृति का प्रसाद"
Blessings of the Mother, Gift of Nature

Our Sacred Commitment

Gou Seva — Service to the Sacred Cow

The cow holds a sacred place in our culture and our hearts. Every purchase you make helps protect and care for our indigenous Gir cows.

🏠

SHELTER

Our Gou Shala provides a safe, loving home for indigenous Gir cows — free to graze and live peacefully.

🌾

NATURAL FOOD

Our cows eat organic fodder and graze on natural pasture. No hormones, no antibiotics — ever.

🩺

HEALTHCARE

Regular veterinary care and Ayurvedic treatments keep our cows healthy and thriving.

🐄

PROTECTION

We rescue and rehabilitate abandoned indigenous cows, protecting their sacred lineage for generations.
